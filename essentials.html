Scheduling an Initial chevron_right
Verify
  • Can I get the account number or phone number to pull up the account?
2 Diagnostics Questions
  • Okay, you want to schedule an Iniitial (subscription name) appointment on your route today?
  • Perfect! Besides that, is there anything else I can take care of for you while you are on the line?
Triple A "Catch All"
  • Thank you so much for helping this customer get this (subscription) set up.
  • (Thank you counts for align.)
  • I can definitely schedule that for you.
Check Autopay & Balance
  • If NOT, let the SP know if they enroll you can apply a $20 credit to their account.
  • If there is an outstanding balance, let the SP know to notify the customer and you can not schedule.
Mention Charging the Fee
  • Just so you and the customer are aware, we are going to collect the Initial Fee of (amount) to the customer while I am scheduling to make sure we have a valid payment method on file.
Hold
  • Do you mind if I put you on a brief hold while I get that scheduled?
  • Thank you. I will be back with you shortly.
While On Hold
  • Check if the agreement was signed (if not, we cannot schedule).
  • Charge Initial Fee: Put limited to Subscription: (Subscription Type). If CX is on autopay and it doesn't go through, do not schedule, If NOT on autopay and doesn't go through, you may still schedule.
  • Schedule Intial
  • (If an Upsell) Cancel old subscriptions with cross-sell.
Take Off Hold
  • Let them know what you did.
  • Alright, I went ahead and put that on your route for today. If you want, go ahead and refresh to make sure it is there.
Outro
  • Is there anything else I can do for you today?
  • After this call you will receive a brief survey through text based on your interaction with me. If you have a moment, I'd appreciate you filling that out.
  • Thank you for calling Pro Support. Have a great day!
