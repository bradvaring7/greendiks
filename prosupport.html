support_agentShared Pro Support Call Flow
chevron_right
  • Intro: “Thank you for calling Pro Support! This is __, who do I have the pleasure of speaking with today?”
  • Build Rapport: “How are you doing today?”
  • Verify Account: Ask for account # or phone # (if needed).
  • Diagnostic Questions:
    • Repeat request back: “So you were needing to ___ today?”
    • “Besides that, is there anything else I can take care of while we’re on the line?”
  • Triple A:
    • Acknowledge: “Got it. You need ___?”
    • Align: “Perfect, thanks for calling this in.”
    • Assure: “I’ll make sure this gets taken care of right away.”
  • Hold Script: “Do you mind if I put you on a brief hold while I take care of that? Thank you so much, I’ll be back shortly.”
  • Outro: “Is there anything else I can take care of for you today?”
assignment_addSetting Up a Lead
chevron_right
  • Gather subscription details (subscription, length, price, rep name).
  • Run Pre-Flight Checklist:
    • Subscription not active
    • No recent lead
    • Autopay check (+$20 credit)
    • Balances
    • Pricing guide
    • Cross-sell reps
  • Set up properly → read back details → send agreement to customer (email/phone).
  • Notes: can’t send to SP, 24-hour rule, mailed agreements process if needed.
event_availableScheduling an Initial
chevron_right
  • Verify agreement signed.
  • Charge Initial Fee (if autopay & fails → don’t schedule).
  • Schedule the initial.
  • If upsell → cancel old subs.
  • Check subscription tab (cancel old, confirm fee, autopay, balances, signed agreement).
  • Script: “Just so you and the customer are aware, we are going to collect that Initial Fee of __ while I’m on hold…”
add_taskAdding Jobs to Routes
chevron_right
  • Ask how many jobs & which branch.
  • While on hold:
    • Use map view (if trained) or recommended routes.
    • Check viability (no red notes, balances >10 days, no escalations).
    • No cherry picking → pick job closest to last job.
  • Confirm back: “I went ahead and added (jobs), go ahead and refresh to confirm.”
