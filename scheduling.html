General Rules
  • No same day appts.
  • After 1, no next day appts.
  • No weekends
  • Max to move appt out is 14 days.
  • No appts if balance is more than $70.
  • Always send notications and mark confirmed.
  • Max hours on a route is 6, only 5 on meeting days (Monday).
  • Max number of appts for a route in top route of the route box.
  • Only 2-hour slots for timed appts, 2-4 is latest allowed.
  • VIP, German Roach, Flea Tick Interior, Termite, Bed Bug require a "S" for SP.
  • Note who gave a approval if permission is required.
  • Don't schedule on empty routes
  • Same day jobs can be added at the SP's request unless there is a Red Note that requires prior notification.
