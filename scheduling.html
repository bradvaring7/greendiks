9-Mile Rulechevron_right
Schedule w/o Permission If Yes to All 3
  • Does the route stay under the duration of 5 hours for a Monday or 6 hours for any other day?
  • Does the distance in the bottom left corner stay under 60 miles?
  • Does the route have at least 2 under 9 without any over 9?
Ask for PTS If
  • If the route has 3 under 9 miles, &
  • No more than 3 over 9 miles
Scheduling Ruleschevron_right
  • 🚫 NO same day appt w/o permission from the BM, SM, Field Support or SP.
  • 💬 Always let SP know when adding same day appt.
  • ❌ No weekends.
  • 🔜 Appt push out max is 2 weeks.
  • 💸 No appts for CXs with balance over $70.
  • 📅 Always send reminder when appt is scheduled.
  • 5️⃣ Monday route duration limit is 5 hrs.
  • 6️⃣ Tue-Fri route duration limit is 6 hrs.
  • 👷🏽‍♂️ VIP, German roach, Tick Flea interior reduction, termite baiting & bed bug can only be service by SPs with a S symbol on their route.
  • 🕗 No 8-10am appts on Mondays.
  • 🚫 Do not schedule on an empty route.
  • 🔐 Once route locked cannot unlock even if it passes rules except for One-Time TFM Offer if adding to existing appt.
  • 👷‍♀️ Same jobs can be added for SPs requests for initial appts or same day job add.
  • 1️⃣ All routes must be have at least one job unreminded left at all times.
  • 📆 Move up next appt date vs reservice appt if within 30 days.
